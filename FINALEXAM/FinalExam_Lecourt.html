NOMADS OF EVE

exploring our distant neighbors

This website was made to help other people explore the fictional world of New Eve.
It includes all the existing empires, ships, and its rich lore

 AMARR EMPIRE

The Amarr Empire, a theocratic monarchy, is the largest nation in New Eden. It is known for its omnipresent religion and use of slave labor.

 CALDARI STATE

A state built on corporate capitalism, the Caldari State is run by a few megacorporations which divide the state between them, controlling and ruling every aspect of society.

 Gallente Federation

The Gallenteans. Self-righteous, meddling, pompous and tiresome, or virile liberalists and defenders of the free world.

 Minmatar Republic

A tough, no-nonsense race, the Minmatar are determined & independent people. Their home, Matar is a paradise, but centuries of abuse have taken its beauty.
